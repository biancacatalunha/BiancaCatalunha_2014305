Welcome to Chorus Cafe Bistro.

Chorus Cafe opened its doors for the first time in 2001. Sirus and his wife trained as italian chefs in Italy in the 1990s, arriving in Ireland to establish and run their own cafe and restaurant.

They knew what they wanted:

To create a homely and welcoming space which offered the best breakfasts and everyday brunch in Dublin, the tastiest lunches and the finest cakes, scones and brownies in the business. Not to mention, of course, the most sublime service too!
