Our Story

Chorus Cafe Bistro opened its doors for the first time in 2001.
Sirus and his wife trained as italian chefs in Italy in the 1990s, arriving in Ireland to establish and run their own cafe and restaurant.

They knew what they wanted:

To create a homely and welcoming space which offered the best breakfasts and everyday brunch in Dublin, the tastiest lunches and the finest cakes, scones and brownies in the business.
Not to mention, of course, the most sublime service too!.

Chorus Cafe bistro quickly established an iconic reputation, and today, fourteen years on, it is still flourishing.
Everything is baked and prepared fresh on the premises;
and Sirus and Amelia still use only the best ingredients:
Illy coffee beans in their coffee machines, free hange eggs, organic products, fresh vegetables, the soups are lovingly made daily.
And now, a perfectly judged wine list is another reason to drop by.

A warm welcome awaits you at Chorus Cafe!
